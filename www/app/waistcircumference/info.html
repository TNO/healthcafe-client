Information about waist circumference

Next to BMI, waist circumference is often used to determine if you have a healthy body composition. The advantage of this measure is that it takes the location of fat tissue into account. Belly fat is unhealthier as compared to fat tissue on your hips.

For women the waist circumference should be between 68 and 80 cm. For men the waist circumference should be between 79 and 94 cm. Is your waist circumference lower? Then you suffer from underweight. Is your waist circumference higher? Then you are overweight.

A too high waist circumference? Try to lose weight.
