Information about BMI

A healthy weight is important. Overweight and obesity increase the chance of lifestyle diseases like diabestes and cardiovascular disease.

The “Body Mass Index”, a ratio between your weight and height, is often used as indicator for a healthy weight.

A BMI between 18,5 and 25 is considered healthy. A lower BMI indicates underweight. With a BMI above 25, you are overweight. Is your BMI over 30? Then you are obese.

Is your BMI to high? Try to reduce weight.
